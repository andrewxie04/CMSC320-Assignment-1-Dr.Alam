Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches by making a new commit that inculdes changes from both branches. This commit preserves the history of the two branches. Rebasing rewrites the history of a branch by moving it to start from another branch. Rebasing makes the project history cleaner and linear but it changes commit history.
